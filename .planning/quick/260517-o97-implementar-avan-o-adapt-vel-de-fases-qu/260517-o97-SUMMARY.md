# Quick Task 260517-o97 Summary

Implemented adaptive phase advancement for action phases:

- `HINT_PHASE` keeps the existing all-hints fast path.
- `GUESS_PHASE` advances when all players guessed or skipped.
- `EXCHANGE_PHASE` advances when no pending/accepted exchange remains and fewer than two players can still exchange.
- `SPY_PHASE` is skipped when nobody is eligible to spy, and advances when all eligible players have attempted.

Also added a `skip_exchange` RPC/Socket.IO/UI action so players can explicitly finish the exchange phase without waiting for the timer.
